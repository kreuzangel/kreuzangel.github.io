About me

I am a graduated geophysicist and a junior researcher working at the Alfred Wegener Institut (AWI) in Bremerhaven, Germany. My research experience focus in remote sensing, data analysis and numerical simulations applied to ice mass changes and linked to climate change in polar regions.

Junior Programmer & Web Developer

I am eager to delve into IT and broad my knowledge in the use of different softwares, programming languages, web development and science communication in a creative and interactive way.

Field work and surveys
